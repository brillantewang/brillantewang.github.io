TeacherPay

A visual analysis of teacher salary to student outcomes across the world

Note: The size of a country bubble represents its GDP.

As a former teacher, I had heard that countries that paid their teachers the highest salaries also had the best education systems.

To see if this was true, I analyzed data provided by the Organization for Economic Cooperation and Development (OECD) to build TeacherPay, a simple tool that shows the relationship between teacher salaries and student outcomes in science, math, and reading across 35 countries.
